**Full voiceover:**
"No imaging tool sees cancer. Each one sees a scale. Electron microscopy and AFM move toward nanoscale structure and surfaces. Fluorescence microscopy localizes proteins and cells. Histology and immunohistochemistry show tissue architecture and markers. MRI shows anatomy and quantitative tissue properties. PET can show molecular or metabolic signal across the body. The question is not which modality is best. The better question is: what scale is the biology, and what signal can answer it?"
