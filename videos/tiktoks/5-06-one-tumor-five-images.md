**Full voiceover:**
"The same tumor looks different depending on the question. MRI shows anatomy and tissue properties. PET shows molecular or metabolic activity across the body. Histology shows tissue architecture: glands, stroma, necrosis, invasion. Immunohistochemistry and fluorescence show markers, cell types, and spatial relationships. Electron microscopy and AFM move toward nanoscale structure and surface properties. None of these is the whole tumor by itself. Each is a projection of the tumor into a measurement system. The science is knowing how to combine them."
