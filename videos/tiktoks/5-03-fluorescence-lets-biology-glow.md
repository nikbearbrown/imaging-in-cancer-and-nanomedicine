**Full voiceover:**
"Fluorescence is how invisible molecules become visible maps. Fluorescent tags make selected molecules emit detectable light. Shine one wavelength in, detect another wavelength out. Different colors can mark nuclei, receptors, immune cells, signaling events, or drug carriers. Under a microscope, that turns molecular identity into location. In research models, fluorescence can also track tumor growth or delivery over time. The color is not decoration. It is a measurement strategy."
